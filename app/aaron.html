The genius of Aaron Swartz

Sat 12 January 2013

In: General

This year has started with very bad news. Aaron Swartz took his own life on January 11.

I did not know Aaron Swartz before his campaign against SOPA. I have never met him in person nor did I know much about him. Yet I am very saddened by what has happened.

Who was Aaron? Simply a genius. He was that genius who wanted to change the world and make it a better place. He has helped in designing the specification of RSS 1.0 back in 2001 when he was only fourteen. He was also the co-founder of Reddit and the creator of web.py. He was no popular money minded person, but he was a knowledge savvy programmer who would do anything for the freedom of knowledge. Anything, you might say? Two years ago, precisely on January 6, Aaron was arrested because he has allegedly downloaded million of articles from JSTOR without permission.

TO CALL Aaron Swartz gifted would be to miss the point. As far as the internet was concerned, he was the gift.
